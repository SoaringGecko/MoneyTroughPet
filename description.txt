								---Money Trough Pet---
Money Trough Pet is a mod that well... makes it so you can equip the Money Trough like a pet.

									---Items---
Money Trough Pet || Can be crafted with either a Money Trough or Piggy Bank at a Work Bench

								---Crafting Changes---
You can turn the Piggy Bank into the Money Trough (and vice versa) using a Work Bench.
